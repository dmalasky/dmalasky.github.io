Paragraph Font: Lato

I chose Lato for the paragraphs because it is a basic and easy to read font.

Normal paragraph example

Before you go any further you need to stop and ask your self, "What are my fitness goals?". Do you want to get bigger muscles, Are you trying to get stronger for a sport, Or are you just trying to decrease your mile time? Focusing in on what outcome your trying to achieve is the key to success when building your workout plan.

Colored paragraph example

Hypertrophy is the process of building muscle. With a Hypertrophy workout you wanna keep the sets lower and the reps mid to high. For the weight you want to go somewhere in between 67%-85% of your 1RM, based on how many reps you choose to do.
